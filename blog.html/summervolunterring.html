My Summer volunteering Experience


Last month I had an opportunity to volunteer in the Young Women In IT even orginized by our college to inspires Young girls to be a part of IT industry. The event was grand. Being a volunteer I had an opportunity to learn lots of new things that I had never done before and meet with lots of new people who wanted to be a part of aspiring field of IT.The experience was very exciting.

My role as a volunteer

  • I had to take the children to the I wing room 101 where they learnt about colour chromatography
  • I had to take the children to the cafeteria where they ate their lunch.
  • I had to take the children to the C wing room 254 where they made how to make bird house.
  • Lastly I had to take them to the robotics class where they played fun games with the roberts.
